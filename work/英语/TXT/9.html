翻译练习1

序号 释义 句子
1 她既聪明又美丽。(as well as) She is clever as well as beautiful.
2 记住离开前关上门。(remember to do) Remember to close the door before you leave.
3 没什么可担心的。(worry about) There is nothing to worry about.
4 我很乐意带你参观潍坊。(take/show...around) I’d like to take/show you around Wei fang.
5 你不该老想着这件事。(keep doing...) You shouldn’t keep thinking about it.
6 你知道怎么打开这架相机吗？(turn on) Do you know how to turn on the camera?
7 冰箱里有些面包和鸡蛋。(There be) There is some bread and eggs in the fridge.
8 我主动要求帮Jack学习数学。(offer to do) I offer to help Jack with his math.
9 直到妈妈回来她才上床睡觉。(not...until...) She didn’t go to bed until her mother came back.
10 很多青少年喜爱运动，比如：足球﹑跑步。(such as) Many teenagers like sports, such as football and running.
11 孩子们放风筝玩的很开心。(have fun doing) The children had a lot of fun flying kites.
12 我们必须避免犯同样的错误。(avoid doing) We must avoid making the same mistake.
13 尽管吵得很,他睡得很好。(in spite of) He slept well in spite of the noise.
14 雨下的如此大,以至于我们不得不呆在家里。(so...that...) It rained so heavily that we had to stay at home.
15 箱子很重,但是他设法搬动了。(manage to do) The box was heavy but he managed to carry it.
16 他们去车站给女儿送行。(see...off) They went to the station to see their daughter off.
17 他根本不喜欢学习数学。(not...at all) He doesn’t like to learn math at all.
18 他给我的杯子斟满了啤酒。(fill...with) He filled my glass with beer.
19 我去过北京两次。(have been to) I have been to Beijing twice.
20 潍坊因风筝而出名。(be famous for) Wei fang is famous for kites.
21 首先,要注意你的健康。（pay attention to） Above all, pay attention to your health.
22 吃大量的水果和蔬菜对健康有益。（be good for） Eating lots of fruit and vegetables is good for health.
23 那些日子,我不想吃任何东西。（feel like） I didn't feel like eating anything in those days.
24 你每天都步行去上学吗？（on foot） Do you go to school on foot every day?
25 我父母对我的决定很满意。（be pleased with） My parents were very pleased with my decision.
26 这箱子太重,这个小男孩搬不动。（too...to...） The box is too heavy for the little boy to carry.
27 顺便问一下,你知道去图书馆怎么走吗？（the way to） By the way, do you know the way to the library?
28 你们班的学生数是多少？（the number of） What is the number of the students in your class?
29 如果你不多做运动，就会发胖。（take exercise） If you don’t take more exercise, you’ll get fat.
30 我们都知道戒烟很难。（give up） We all know it’s very difficult to give up smoking.
31 妈妈将会说服他把药吃下去。（persuade...to...） His mother will persuade him to take the medicine.
32 你不该抽烟！它对健康不利。（be bad for） You shouldn’t smoke! It’s bad for your health.
33 汉语和英语之间有很多不同之处。（between...and...） There are many differences between Chinese and English.
34 听英文歌能帮助学生学习英语。（help...with） Listening to English songs can help students with their English.
35 昨天我们花了两个小时到达那儿。（take...to...） It took us two hours to get there yesterday.
