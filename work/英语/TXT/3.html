A

序号 单词 释义 例句 例句翻译
1 account for 说明，解释 How do you account for his change of attitude? 你怎样来解释他态度的变化？
2 accuse …of 指控……，谴责…… I don't think anyone can accuse him of not being frank. 我看谁也不能说他不坦率。
3 adapt (…) to… 使适应 You should adapt yourself to the new environment. 你应该适应新环境。
4 add to 增添 This lesson adds to the value of the book. 这一课增加了这本书的价值。
5 add up to 合计达 For a hit show, profits can add up to millions of dollars. 一场热门演出的利润可达数百万美元。
6 adjust (…) to （使）适应 Some animals adjust themselves to their environment by instinct. 有些动物会本能地适应环境。
7 agree to 同意某人观点、意见 I find it impossible to agree to your terms. 我无法接受你的条件。
8 agree with… 同意……的意见；适合 That shows she did not quite agree with me. 这表示她不同意我的意见。
9 ahead of time/schedule 提前 We managed to fulfill the task ahead of time. 我们总算提前完成了任务。
10 appeal to 呼吁；吸引 Does the idea of working abroad appeal to you? 你对去国外工作感兴趣吗？
11 apply for 申请 They apply for permission to use the tennis court. 他们申请借用这个网球场。
12 apply … to… 把……应用于…… In this way they can better apply theory to practice. 这样他们就能更好地把理论运用到实践中去。
13 approve of 赞成 I don't approve of your way of looking at things. 我不赞成你看事情的方法。
14 as a matter of fact 事实上 As a matter of fact, everything has developed as we wanted. 事实上，情况的发展和我们希望的完全一样。
15 as a result 作为结果；因此 As a result, he was given an excellent job. 结果他得到了一份好工作。
16 as a whole 作为整体，就整体来看 We should analyze the problem as a whole. 我们应该把问题作为一个整体来分析。
17 as far as 就……而言，远到……，到……程度（表示程度，范围） As far as I know, he will be away for three months. 据我所知, 他将离开三个月。
18 as usual 照常 Everything went on as usual as though nothing had happened. 一切照常进行，好像什么事也没有发生。
19 ask a favor of 请……帮个忙 There's just one last favor I need to ask of you. 还有最后一件事要你帮忙。
20 associate A with B 把A和B联系起来 What do you associate with such a heavy snow? 对这样一场大雪你有什么联想？
21 at a loss 茫然不知所措 The man looked surprised and somewhat at a loss. 那人看起来非常吃惊，也有点手足无措。
22 at all costs / at the cost of 不惜一切代价 We are determined to defend our motherland at all costs. 我们决心不惜任何代价保卫祖国。
23 at present 现在，目前 We are deadly short of staff at present. 目前我们极缺职员。
24 at the cost of 以……为代价 The young policeman saved the child at the cost of his own life. 那位年轻的警察为了拯救这个小孩，而牺牲了自己的生命。
25 at the moment 此刻，目前；那时，当时 Our chief concern at the moment is the weather. 眼前我们最关心的事是天气。
26 at the risk of 冒着生命危险 They were determined to get there even at the risk of their life. 他们决心要去，即使冒着生命危险。
27 at the same time 同时 They both spoke at the same time. 他们同时说话。
28 attach … to 使附着，使依附；把……连接在……上 He attached the stamp to the letter. 他把邮票粘在信上。
29 attend to 处理，照料 He is busy attending to his duties. 他忙于处理他的职务。
30 be about to 即将，正要 I was about to leave when you phoned. 我正要走时，你打电话来了。

B

序号 短语 中文解释 例句 中文例句
31 base ... on ... 把……放在……的基础上 We must base ourselves on the interests of the people. 我们一定要从人民的利益出发。
32 be absent from 缺席 You must not be absent from the meetings without cause. 你必须参加那些会议，不得无故缺席。
33 be absorbed in... 全神贯注于…… She sat on the ground absorbed in her studies. 她坐在地上专心学习。
34 be accustomed to doing 习惯于做 English people are accustomed to driving on the left. 英国人习惯于靠左边开车。
35 be anxious about 担忧 There is no reason to be anxious about the result. 不必为这结果而担心。
36 be aware of 意识到的 We should at all times be aware of our own shortcomings. 我们必须随时注意自己的缺点。
37 be capable of 有能力 Be ready to assemble at the first call and be capable of fighting and winning. 召之即来，来之能战，战之能胜。
38 be composed of... 由……组成 The board shall be composed of eight directors. 董事会由八名董事组成。
39 be concerned about 关心 He is more concerned about others than about himself. 他关心别人比关心自己还重。
40 be confident of 对……有信心 I have every reason to be confident of our victory. 我有充分的理由坚信胜利是属于我们的。
41 be contrary to 与……相反 Contrary to our expectation, the examination turned out to be a piece of cake. 出乎我们预料的是这次考试容易极了。
42 be curious about 对……好奇 I'm most curious about how they achieved their success. 我很想知道他们是如何获得成功的。
43 be determined to do 决定做某事 Let us be determined to be free of problems that do not exist. 让我们下定决心，由那些根本就不存在的问题中脱身。
44 be engaged in 忙于做某事 His love to language causes him to be engaged in translation. 他对语言的热爱促使他从事翻译工作。
45 be equal to 与……相等 Will she be equal to such a task? 她能胜任这样一项工作吗？
46 be exposed to 曝光于，接触 Poor John was exposed to the wind and rain. 可怜的约翰处于风雨交加之中。
47 be faced with sth. 面对…… Sometimes people are faced with unfortunate circumstances that cannot be changed. 有时候，人们会面临无法改变的不幸。
48 be familiar with 对……熟悉 Since you are familiar with the field, we know your views will be extremely interesting to us. 您对此领域很熟悉，您的见解定会给我们带来很大兴趣。
49 be fed up with 对……厌烦 I am simply fed up with this kind of weather. 我简直对这种天气烦透了。
50 be fit for 适合…… I ought to be fit for work at the end of the month. 到月底时，我应该可以工作了。
51 be free from 没有……的 She had the good fortune to be free from illness all her life. 她真幸运，一生没有病痛。
52 be in favor of 支持（赞成）…… You would be in favor of our proposal, wouldn't you? 你赞成我们的提议，是吗？
53 be independent of 独立于 If you have a car, you will be independent of trains and buses. 你要是有辆汽车就不受火车和公车的支配了。
54 be inferior to 较低于，与……相比不如 I consider this one to be inferior to that one. 我认为这个比那个要差。
55 be involved in 卷入，参与 You will be well advised not to get involved in their quarrel. 您最好不要卷入他们的争吵。
56 be located (in) 坐落（在） Shenzhen is located in South China bordering on Hong Kong. 深圳位于华南，比邻香港。
57 be of good/poor quality 质量好/差 Coffee from Brazil is usually of good quality. 巴西的咖啡通常质量很好。
58 be on a diet 节食 She is on a diet and is losing weight. 她节制饮食，体重一直在下降。
59 be opposed to doing 强烈反对做某事 She seems very much opposed to your going abroad. 她好像很反对你出国。
60 be particular about 对……挑剔 You shouldn't be so particular about what you wear. 你不该对穿着这么讲究。
61 be popular with / among 受某人欢迎 Fifty years ago nobody could expect that computers would be popular with the family in China. 五十年前没人能预料到上海的家庭会普及电脑。
62 be qualified for 可胜任…… In my estimation, the applicant should be well qualified for this job. 据我估计，这位应聘者完全具备这项工作的条件。
63 be related to 与……相关 A relative means a person to whom one is related. 亲戚的意思就是有血缘关系的人。
64 be responsible for 对……负责任 Parents are supposed to be responsible to their children for their education. 父母应当对其子女的教育负有责任。
65 be satisfied with 对……感到满意 My impression is that they are satisfied with you performance. 我的印象是，他们对你的表现很满意。
66 be second to none 第一，不次于任何人 In mathematics, he is second to none in the class. 就数学来说，他是班里第一。
67 be short of 缺乏 The trouble is that we are short of hand. 困难是我们缺少人手。
68 be similar to 与……相似 His dress is very similar to mine in colour. 他衣服的颜色和我衣服的很相似。
69 be strict with 对……严格 Is it necessary for you to be so strict with his children? 你有必要那样严格地对待他的孩子们吗？
70 be suitable for 适合…… There is no single plastic material which is suitable for all applications. 没有哪一种塑料材料是万能的。
71 be superior to 优于…… You must give up the idea that you are superior to them in every respect. 你必须放弃认为自己在各方面都优于他们的想法。
72 be sure of 坚信；确信 It is said she is quite sure of success. 据说她很有成功的把握。
73 be worried about 为……担心 There's really no reason to be worried about what will happen tomorrow. 确实没有理由为明天的事情担心。
74 be / get used to 习惯于…… It takes time to get used to new shoes. 穿惯新鞋需要时间。
75 bear ... in mind 把……牢记在心 The students were asked to bear in mind what teachers told in class. 要求学生记住老师在课堂上所讲的内容。
76 begin with 由……开始 We think it necessary to begin with the experiment. 我们认为先做这个实验是必要的。
77 behave oneself 行为检点、守规矩 You should educate your children to behave themselves. 你应该教育你的孩子们守规矩。
78 believe in 信仰；对……有信心 I believe in your ability to solve the problem. 我相信你有解决这问题的能力。
79 believe it or not 信不信由你 Believe it or not, it was a hug that changed my mind. 信不信由你，是一个拥抱改变了我的心情。
80 benefit from 得益于 Who will benefit from the old man's death? 那位老人死后，谁将是受益人？
81 blame ... for 因……责备某人 The driver is partially to blame for the accident. 司机对那次车祸应负部分责任。
82 break away from 脱离，逃跑 You must break away from such bad habits. 你必须革除这样的坏习惯。
83 break down 抛锚；倒塌；中止 Our car broke down and we had to draw it to a garage. 我们的车坏了, 不得不把它拖到修车厂去。
84 break out 爆发 The forecast that war would break out proved true. 战争会爆发的预测被证实了。
85 break the law 违法 We can not just sit by while these people break the law. 我们不能坐视这些人违犯法律。
86 break through 突破；突围 It was difficult at first to break through her quiet manner. 起初她很难克服那内向的性格。
87 bring ... to an end 结束 Only those who know the underlying truth can bring this to an end. 只有那些知道背后真相的人可以结束这一切.
88 bring ... to life 使……苏醒 It is said that the doctor is able to bring the dying back to life. 据说这位医生能起死回生。
89 bring about 引起 Science has brought about many changes in our lives. 科学为我们生活带来了很多变化。
90 bring up 抚养 He gave much attention and thought to bring up his children. 他在教育孩子方面花了大量的心血。
91 build up 建立，树立 The goalkeeper is organizing to build up a wall. 守门员正在组织筑人墙。
92 burst into 突然发作；突然……起来 They were so moved that they burst into tears. 他们感动得哭了起来。
93 by accident 意外地；偶然 We met by accident at the railway station. 我们在火车站偶然相遇。
94 by all means 一定 The prices are variable, shop around by all means. 价格高低不一，所以一定要货比三家逐店选购。
95 by comparison 比较起来，相比之下 By comparison, trips by sea offer a great variety of civilized comforts. 相比较而言，海上旅行可提供各种文明的舒适条件。
96 by means of 用，依靠 We express our thoughts by means of words. 我们用话语来表达思想。
97 by mistake 弄错 He restored the umbrella he had taken by mistake. 他把拿错了的雨伞放回原处。
98 by no means 决不 By no means can theory be separated from practice. 理论决不能脱离实践。
99 by this means 用这种方法 Only by this means can you make greater progress in your work. 只有这样你才能在工作中取得更大的进步。
100 by / in contrast 对比之下 His white hair was in sharp contrast to his dank skin. 他的白发同他的黑皮肤形成鲜明的对比。

C

序号 短语 中文解释 例句 中文例句
101 call for 要求；需要 More work doesn't necessarily call for more men. 增加工作不一定要增加人员。
102 call off 取消 What is the alternative to call the trip off? 如果取消这次旅游，那我们还有什么地方可去？
103 care about 关心、介意 They don't care about the expenses. 他们对费用不在乎。
104 carry on 继续；进行 We must try to carry on as if nothing had happened. 我们必须继续下去, 只当作什么都没有发生过。
105 carry out 执行；实施 We expect him to carry out his promises. 我们期待他履行诺言。
106 catch one's eye 引起注意 Let's find a table first and then try to catch a waiter's eye. 我们先找张桌子坐下，再叫个服务员过来。
107 catch up with 赶上 Excessive drinking and smoking will catch up with you. 过度喝酒和抽烟迟早会使你受害不浅。
108 catch / lose sight of 看见/看不见 Don't let me catch sight of you doing it again! 别让我再看到你干这种事了！
109 charge ... with 控告 The police are going to charge him with murder. 警察要控告他杀人。
110 cheer up 使高兴；高兴起来 Try to cheer up. Things are not so bad as they seem. 想办法高兴点儿, 情况并不像看上去那样糟。
111 clean up 打扫干净 This room needs cleaning up. 这间房间需要打扫一下。
112 combine ... with ... 把……和……结合起来 It is important that we combine theory with practice. 对我们来说，把实践和理论结合起来是重要的。
113 come across 不期而遇 He is the most direct young man I`ve ever come across. 他是我碰到过的最直率的青年。
114 come out 出来；开花；结果是 Have you get the issue that come out recently? 你有最近出的那一期杂志吗？
115 come to (oneself) 达到；共计；恢复知觉，苏醒 I was already on my way when it suddenly came to me that I hadn't hung out my washing. 我上路以后才想起我没有把洗完的衣服晾起来。
116 come to / arrive at a conclusion 得出结论 I sincerely hope that our negotiation will come to a successful conclusion. 我衷心希望这次谈判能圆满成功。
117 come up with 提出 Is this the very best you can come up with? 这就是你能提供的最好的办法吗？
118 come up 走近；长出来 While we were talking, a man came up. 我们正在谈话时, 一个人走了过来。
119 comment on 评论 The teacher refused to comment on the examination results. 教师不肯评论考试结果。
120 commit a crime 犯罪 If you commit a crime you can never escape being punished. 你要是犯罪，就逃脱不了要受惩罚。
121 common knowledge 常识 It's common knowledge that women talk more than men. 众所周知，女人总比男人说话多。
122 compare A to B 把A比喻成B Shakespeare compared the world to a stage. 莎士比亚把世界比作舞台。
123 compare A with B 把A和B比较 Few writers can compare with him in that respect. 在这方面很少作家能和他相比。
124 compete with 与……相竞争 No student in our class can compete in English with Mary. 在英语方面，我们班上没有人能与玛丽竞争。
125 concentrate on 集中精力在…… We must concentrate our efforts on finding ways to reduce costs. 我们要集中精力找出降低成本的办法。
126 conduct a survey 做一个调查 If we want a final conclusion, we must conduct specialized survey research. 如果要下定论，必须进行专门的调查研究。
127 confuse A with B 把A和B混淆 I always confuse John with his brother, for they are very much alike. 我总是分不清约翰和他的哥哥，他们长得太像了。
128 congratulate sb. on sth. 因某事祝贺某人 You can congratulate yourself on having done a good job. 你的工作做得很出色，你应该感到自豪。
129 consider ... (as / to be) 把……看作 I consider it a great honor to be invited to dinner. 我认为能被邀请参加晚宴是很大的荣幸。
130 consist of 由……组成 Water is said to consist of oxygen and hydrogen. 据说水由氢气和氧合成。
131 contribute to 有助于 Various factors contributed to his success. 多种因素导致了他的成功。
132 convince sb. of sth. 使某人相信某事 We must convince the students of the need for wider reading. 我们必须让学生们认识到广泛的阅读的必要性。
133 cooperate with sb. 和某人合作 If you can cooperate with each other, everything should run smoothly. 如果你们能互相配合，一切都会顺利的。
134 cope with 对付，应付，处理 How do you cope with the problem of poor vision? 怎样解决视力不好这一难题呢？
135 count on 依靠，指望 That was the only thing they could count on. 那是他们唯一依靠的东西。
136 cover a long distance 走了很长一段路 We estimate ( that) it will take three days to cover the distance. 我们估计走完这段路程要花三天时间。
137 cut off 切断；断绝 His dishonesty cut him off from all of us. 他为人的不诚实使我们大家都不愿与之为伍。

D

138 date back to 追溯到 There are few people who would believe that these books date back to the 15th century. 很少有人相信这种书的历史竟可以追溯到15世纪。
139 devote oneself to doing sth. 献身于做某事 After she graduated, she continued to devote herself to research. 毕业后她继续致力于研究工作。
140 die down 逐渐平息 The gossip will soon die down now that the woman is dead. 既然这名妇女已经死了，这些闲言碎语也将很快消失。
141 die out 灭绝 Those species inadaptable to changing conditions may die out. 那些不能适应变化中的环境的物种可能会灭绝。
142 differ from 与……不同 The brothers differ from each other in their interests. 这几个兄弟各有所好。
143 distinguish ... from ... 把……区别于 It is hard for young children to distinguish right from wrong. 对于小孩来说，分辨是非是困难的。
144 divide ... into ... 把……分成 First he divided it in two, and then divided each into four. 他首先把它分成两份, 然后又把每份分成四份。
145 do harm / good to 对……有害处/好处 Lies also do harm to those who tell them. 谎话对说谎者本身也有损害。
146 do nothing but do 除了……什么也不做 You can do nothing but let matters take their course. 你无能为力，只能顺其自然。
147 draw / form / reach / make a conclusion 得出结论 What conclusion can we draw from their leave? 他们的离去能使我们得出什么结论呢？
148 draw / attract one's attention 吸引某人的注意 She gave a quiet cough to attract my attention. 她轻轻地咳了一声好引起我注意。
149 drop in on / at 顺便拜访 Be sure to drop in on me when you can spare a moment. 等你抽得出空时一定别忘了顺便来看看我。
150 due to 由于；因为 Her absence is due to illness. 她因病缺席。

E

151 end up with 以……告终 We had a banquet, which ended (up) with soup. 我们举行了一个宴会，最后一道是喝汤。
152 enter for / sign up for 报名参加 Are you going to enter yourself for the contest? 你打算报名参加比赛吗？
153 equip sb. with sth. 用……来装备某人 Education must equip students with the skills and the information needed to make sense of reality. 教育须用必需的技巧和信息装备学生，使之懂得真理。
154 escape from 从……逃离 Young people often desire to escape from their parents. 年轻人常常想要摆脱他们的父母。
155 exchange A for B 用A交换B Where can I exchange my dollars for pounds? 我可以在哪里把美金换成英镑？

F

156 face up to 勇敢面对 There are dangers which you must face up to. 有危险，你必须勇敢地面对。
157 fall in love with ... 爱上某人 Women don't fall in love as easily as men do. 女士不像男士那样快地坠入情网。
158 fall off 跌落 Did she fall off the cliff by accident, or was she pushed off? 她是意外地掉下悬崖(cliff)，还是被推下去的呢？
159 far from enough 远远不够 We have made some progress, but it is far from enough. 我们已经取得了一些进步，但还远远不够。
160 fight against 与……斗争 He tried to fight against being transferred to another department. 他试图反对被调往另一部门。
161 figure out 算出，推测出 No one can figure out how the fire started. 没有人弄得清这场火灾是怎么引发的。
162 fill ... with 用……充满 The sight of him filled her with happiness. 一见到他，她心里就充满了幸福。
163 find an excuse 找借口 Tom can not find a good excuse for being late. 汤姆找不到充分的理由说明迟到的原因。
164 find fault with 挑……毛病 I can't find fault with the new restaurant: the food is good and the service is excellent. 我无法对那家新餐馆吹毛求疵：食品很好，服务也是一流的。
165 focus (one's attention) on 集中注意 A near-sighted person cannot focus accurately on distant objects. 一个近视的人不能够准确地调整焦距以看到远处物体。
166 follow one's example 学某人的样子 We must all follow his example and serve the people heart and soul. 我们都应学习他的榜样，全心全意为人民服务。
167 for free 免费地；无偿地 I got the sample for free. 我免费得到这样品。
168 for the sake of 为了……利益 She sacrificed her life for the sake of freedom. 她为争取自由而献出了自己的生命。
169 forgive sb. for 因……而原谅某人 I hope you forgive me for opening your letter by mistake. 我误拆了您的信，期望您原谅。
