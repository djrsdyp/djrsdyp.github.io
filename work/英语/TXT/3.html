高中英语基本词组及参考例句

A

序号 单词 释义 例句 例句翻译
1 account for 说明，解释 How do you account for his change of attitude? 你怎样来解释他态度的变化？
2 accuse …of 指控……，谴责…… I don't think anyone can accuse him of not being frank. 我看谁也不能说他不坦率。
3 adapt (…) to… 使适应 You should adapt yourself to the new environment. 你应该适应新环境。
4 add to 增添 This lesson adds to the value of the book. 这一课增加了这本书的价值。
5 add up to 合计达 For a hit show, profits can add up to millions of dollars. 一场热门演出的利润可达数百万美元。
6 adjust (…) to （使）适应 Some animals adjust themselves to their environment by instinct. 有些动物会本能地适应环境。
7 agree to 同意某人观点、意见 I find it impossible to agree to your terms. 我无法接受你的条件。
8 agree with… 同意……的意见；适合 That shows she did not quite agree with me. 这表示她不同意我的意见。
9 ahead of time/schedule 提前 We managed to fulfill the task ahead of time. 我们总算提前完成了任务。
10 appeal to 呼吁；吸引 Does the idea of working abroad appeal to you? 你对去国外工作感兴趣吗？
11 apply for 申请 They apply for permission to use the tennis court. 他们申请借用这个网球场。
12 apply … to… 把……应用于…… In this way they can better apply theory to practice. 这样他们就能更好地把理论运用到实践中去。
13 approve of 赞成 I don't approve of your way of looking at things. 我不赞成你看事情的方法。
14 as a matter of fact 事实上 As a matter of fact, everything has developed as we wanted. 事实上，情况的发展和我们希望的完全一样。
15 as a result 作为结果；因此 As a result, he was given an excellent job. 结果他得到了一份好工作。
16 as a whole 作为整体，就整体来看 We should analyze the problem as a whole. 我们应该把问题作为一个整体来分析。
17 as far as 就……而言，远到……，到……程度（表示程度，范围） As far as I know, he will be away for three months. 据我所知, 他将离开三个月。
18 as usual 照常 Everything went on as usual as though nothing had happened. 一切照常进行，好像什么事也没有发生。
19 ask a favor of 请……帮个忙 There's just one last favor I need to ask of you. 还有最后一件事要你帮忙。
20 associate A with B 把A和B联系起来 What do you associate with such a heavy snow? 对这样一场大雪你有什么联想？
21 at a loss 茫然不知所措 The man looked surprised and somewhat at a loss. 那人看起来非常吃惊，也有点手足无措。
22 at all costs / at the cost of 不惜一切代价 We are determined to defend our motherland at all costs. 我们决心不惜任何代价保卫祖国。
23 at present 现在，目前 We are deadly short of staff at present. 目前我们极缺职员。
24 at the cost of 以……为代价 The young policeman saved the child at the cost of his own life. 那位年轻的警察为了拯救这个小孩，而牺牲了自己的生命。
25 at the moment 此刻，目前；那时，当时 Our chief concern at the moment is the weather. 眼前我们最关心的事是天气。
26 at the risk of 冒着生命危险 They were determined to get there even at the risk of their life. 他们决心要去，即使冒着生命危险。
27 at the same time 同时 They both spoke at the same time. 他们同时说话。
28 attach … to 使附着，使依附；把……连接在……上 He attached the stamp to the letter. 他把邮票粘在信上。
29 attend to 处理，照料 He is busy attending to his duties. 他忙于处理他的职务。
30 be about to 即将，正要 I was about to leave when you phoned. 我正要走时，你打电话来了。
