A

序号 单词 释义 例句 例句翻译
1 account for 说明，解释 How do you account for his change of attitude? 你怎样来解释他态度的变化？
2 accuse …of 指控……，谴责…… I don't think anyone can accuse him of not being frank. 我看谁也不能说他不坦率。
3 adapt (…) to… 使适应 You should adapt yourself to the new environment. 你应该适应新环境。
4 add to 增添 This lesson adds to the value of the book. 这一课增加了这本书的价值。
5 add up to 合计达 For a hit show, profits can add up to millions of dollars. 一场热门演出的利润可达数百万美元。
6 adjust (…) to （使）适应 Some animals adjust themselves to their environment by instinct. 有些动物会本能地适应环境。
7 agree to 同意某人观点、意见 I find it impossible to agree to your terms. 我无法接受你的条件。
8 agree with… 同意……的意见；适合 That shows she did not quite agree with me. 这表示她不同意我的意见。
9 ahead of time/schedule 提前 We managed to fulfill the task ahead of time. 我们总算提前完成了任务。
10 appeal to 呼吁；吸引 Does the idea of working abroad appeal to you? 你对去国外工作感兴趣吗？
11 apply for 申请 They apply for permission to use the tennis court. 他们申请借用这个网球场。
12 apply … to… 把……应用于…… In this way they can better apply theory to practice. 这样他们就能更好地把理论运用到实践中去。
13 approve of 赞成 I don't approve of your way of looking at things. 我不赞成你看事情的方法。
14 as a matter of fact 事实上 As a matter of fact, everything has developed as we wanted. 事实上，情况的发展和我们希望的完全一样。
15 as a result 作为结果；因此 As a result, he was given an excellent job. 结果他得到了一份好工作。
16 as a whole 作为整体，就整体来看 We should analyze the problem as a whole. 我们应该把问题作为一个整体来分析。
17 as far as 就……而言，远到……，到……程度（表示程度，范围） As far as I know, he will be away for three months. 据我所知, 他将离开三个月。
18 as usual 照常 Everything went on as usual as though nothing had happened. 一切照常进行，好像什么事也没有发生。
19 ask a favor of 请……帮个忙 There's just one last favor I need to ask of you. 还有最后一件事要你帮忙。
20 associate A with B 把A和B联系起来 What do you associate with such a heavy snow? 对这样一场大雪你有什么联想？
21 at a loss 茫然不知所措 The man looked surprised and somewhat at a loss. 那人看起来非常吃惊，也有点手足无措。
22 at all costs / at the cost of 不惜一切代价 We are determined to defend our motherland at all costs. 我们决心不惜任何代价保卫祖国。
23 at present 现在，目前 We are deadly short of staff at present. 目前我们极缺职员。
24 at the cost of 以……为代价 The young policeman saved the child at the cost of his own life. 那位年轻的警察为了拯救这个小孩，而牺牲了自己的生命。
25 at the moment 此刻，目前；那时，当时 Our chief concern at the moment is the weather. 眼前我们最关心的事是天气。
26 at the risk of 冒着生命危险 They were determined to get there even at the risk of their life. 他们决心要去，即使冒着生命危险。
27 at the same time 同时 They both spoke at the same time. 他们同时说话。
28 attach … to 使附着，使依附；把……连接在……上 He attached the stamp to the letter. 他把邮票粘在信上。
29 attend to 处理，照料 He is busy attending to his duties. 他忙于处理他的职务。
30 be about to 即将，正要 I was about to leave when you phoned. 我正要走时，你打电话来了。

B

序号 短语 中文解释 例句 中文例句
31 base ... on ... 把……放在……的基础上 We must base ourselves on the interests of the people. 我们一定要从人民的利益出发。
32 be absent from 缺席 You must not be absent from the meetings without cause. 你必须参加那些会议，不得无故缺席。
33 be absorbed in... 全神贯注于…… She sat on the ground absorbed in her studies. 她坐在地上专心学习。
34 be accustomed to doing 习惯于做 English people are accustomed to driving on the left. 英国人习惯于靠左边开车。
35 be anxious about 担忧 There is no reason to be anxious about the result. 不必为这结果而担心。
36 be aware of 意识到的 We should at all times be aware of our own shortcomings. 我们必须随时注意自己的缺点。
37 be capable of 有能力 Be ready to assemble at the first call and be capable of fighting and winning. 召之即来，来之能战，战之能胜。
38 be composed of... 由……组成 The board shall be composed of eight directors. 董事会由八名董事组成。
39 be concerned about 关心 He is more concerned about others than about himself. 他关心别人比关心自己还重。
40 be confident of 对……有信心 I have every reason to be confident of our victory. 我有充分的理由坚信胜利是属于我们的。
41 be contrary to 与……相反 Contrary to our expectation, the examination turned out to be a piece of cake. 出乎我们预料的是这次考试容易极了。
42 be curious about 对……好奇 I'm most curious about how they achieved their success. 我很想知道他们是如何获得成功的。
43 be determined to do 决定做某事 Let us be determined to be free of problems that do not exist. 让我们下定决心，由那些根本就不存在的问题中脱身。
44 be engaged in 忙于做某事 His love to language causes him to be engaged in translation. 他对语言的热爱促使他从事翻译工作。
45 be equal to 与……相等 Will she be equal to such a task? 她能胜任这样一项工作吗？
46 be exposed to 曝光于，接触 Poor John was exposed to the wind and rain. 可怜的约翰处于风雨交加之中。
47 be faced with sth. 面对…… Sometimes people are faced with unfortunate circumstances that cannot be changed. 有时候，人们会面临无法改变的不幸。
48 be familiar with 对……熟悉 Since you are familiar with the field, we know your views will be extremely interesting to us. 您对此领域很熟悉，您的见解定会给我们带来很大兴趣。
49 be fed up with 对……厌烦 I am simply fed up with this kind of weather. 我简直对这种天气烦透了。
50 be fit for 适合…… I ought to be fit for work at the end of the month. 到月底时，我应该可以工作了。
51 be free from 没有……的 She had the good fortune to be free from illness all her life. 她真幸运，一生没有病痛。
52 be in favor of 支持（赞成）…… You would be in favor of our proposal, wouldn't you? 你赞成我们的提议，是吗？
53 be independent of 独立于 If you have a car, you will be independent of trains and buses. 你要是有辆汽车就不受火车和公车的支配了。
54 be inferior to 较低于，与……相比不如 I consider this one to be inferior to that one. 我认为这个比那个要差。
55 be involved in 卷入，参与 You will be well advised not to get involved in their quarrel. 您最好不要卷入他们的争吵。
56 be located (in) 坐落（在） Shenzhen is located in South China bordering on Hong Kong. 深圳位于华南，比邻香港。
57 be of good/poor quality 质量好/差 Coffee from Brazil is usually of good quality. 巴西的咖啡通常质量很好。
58 be on a diet 节食 She is on a diet and is losing weight. 她节制饮食，体重一直在下降。
59 be opposed to doing 强烈反对做某事 She seems very much opposed to your going abroad. 她好像很反对你出国。
60 be particular about 对……挑剔 You shouldn't be so particular about what you wear. 你不该对穿着这么讲究。
61 be popular with / among 受某人欢迎 Fifty years ago nobody could expect that computers would be popular with the family in China. 五十年前没人能预料到上海的家庭会普及电脑。
62 be qualified for 可胜任…… In my estimation, the applicant should be well qualified for this job. 据我估计，这位应聘者完全具备这项工作的条件。
63 be related to 与……相关 A relative means a person to whom one is related. 亲戚的意思就是有血缘关系的人。
64 be responsible for 对……负责任 Parents are supposed to be responsible to their children for their education. 父母应当对其子女的教育负有责任。
65 be satisfied with 对……感到满意 My impression is that they are satisfied with you performance. 我的印象是，他们对你的表现很满意。
